class panobbgo_lib.classic.RosenbrockStochastic(dims, par1=100, jitter=0.1)[source]¶

Bases: panobbgo_lib.lib.Problem

Stochastic variant of Rosenbrock function.

\[f(x) = \sum_i (\mathit{par}_1 \mathit{eps}_i (x_{i+1} - x_i^2)^2 + (1-x_i)^2)\]

where \(\mathit{eps}_i\) is a uniformly random (n-1)-dimensional vector in \(\left[0, 1\right)^{n-1}\).
